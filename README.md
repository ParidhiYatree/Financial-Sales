# Financial-Sales
•	Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales, profit, and product performance across regions and segments.
•	Created DAX measures to calculate KPIs such as Total Revenue, Total Profit, and Discount Impact.
•	Designed visualizations to track sales trends by product, country, and time period.
•	Performed data cleaning, transformation, and data modeling for accurate reporting.
Outcome: Enabled identification of top-performing products and regions, improved sales trend monitoring, and supported data-driven pricing and business decisions.
Tools: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling, Data Visualization
